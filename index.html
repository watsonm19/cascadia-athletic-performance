About Us

Salmon Creek, Vancouver, WA

Cascadia Athletic Performance helps athletes and active individuals reach their peak through results-driven training in a personalized one-on-one setting.

We specialize in sport-specific development for football, soccer, basketball, lacrosse, and volleyball utilizing the Functional Patterns methodology — with a strong emphasis on injury prevention, movement efficiency, and total-body athleticism.

Our method blends science-backed training with real-world experience to build proper core strength, joint stability, and body symmetry. We also reinforce weak-side awareness and dynamic movement skills that translate directly to sport.

Ideal for athletes of all levels, ages 11 and up.
